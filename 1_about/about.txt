[Design and  Machine Learning: Automation Takes Command?](https://www.hesge.ch/head/projet/design-et-machine-learning-lautomatisation-au-pouvoir) is a research project of Geneva University of Art and Design ([HEAD – Genève](https://www.hesge.ch/head), [HES-SO](https://www.hes-so.ch/accueil)) in 2022-2023. 

- Applicant: [Anthony Masure](https://www.anthonymasure.com)
- Interviews: [Alexia Mathieu](https://www.hesge.ch/head/annuaire/alexia-mathieu)
- Design & Development: [Douglas Edric Stanley](https://abstractmachine.net)
- Posters: [E+K](http://www.e-k.fr) (Élise Gay & Kevin Donnot)

(select:1)This experimental tool illustrates subjects discussed in the research project(deselect:). The generators indicate original text with the colors red or green, followed by the generated text in violet. Text is generated using <span class='purple'>[gpt-3.5-turbo](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/gpt-3-5)</span>. Keywords from the original text are converted into a prompt using various natural language techniques, and is then generated into an image via <span class='purple'>[DALL·E](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/images)</span>.
